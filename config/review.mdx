## Setting up Reviews
* Run ``/settings config``

    - Click **continue** until you reach panel **4**
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Reviewer Role**, enter a role that can review designers
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Channel to log reviews**, enter a channel that all Reviews will be sent to

You have now setup the Reviews Module!
