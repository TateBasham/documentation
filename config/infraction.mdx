## Setting up Infractions
* Run ``/settings config``

    - Click **continue** until you reach panel **3**
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Role**, enter a role that can use Infractions
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Channel**, enter a channel that all Infractions will be logged to

You have now setup the Infractions Module!
