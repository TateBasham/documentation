## Setting up Orders
* Run ``/settings config``

    - Click **continue** until you reach panel **5**
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Designer Role**, enter a role that can log orders
    - In the dropdown that says **Select a Channel to log orders**, enter a channel that all Orders will be sent to

You have now setup the Orders Module!
