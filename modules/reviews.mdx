### Command: `/review add`
Adds a review to the designer after completing the order! 

#### **Parameters**  
- **`designer`** (required) – The designer you wish to review.
- **`product`** (required) – The product you recieved from the order, which you are reviewing.
- **`review`** (required) – Select the stars (out of 5) you wish to give the designer!
- **`notes`** (optional) - Additional notes about the order you want to give!
